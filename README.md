Spendly

A student expense tracker I built to stop wondering where my money went every month. No backend, no login, no nonsense — just open it and start tracking.

[Live Demo →](https://mindinloop.github.io/spendly)

---

Why I built this

I was using a notes app to track expenses. It was terrible. Spreadsheets felt like overkill. Every budgeting app wanted my bank details or a subscription. So I spent a weekend building exactly what I needed something fast, offline-first, and actually usable on a phone.

---

What it does

- Set a monthly budget and watch a progress bar slowly judge you
- Add expenses with a category, emoji, and optional note
- See spending broken down as a donut chart and a 14-day bar chart
- Search, filter by today/this week/all time, and sort by amount or date
- Export the month's data as a CSV for your own records
- Create custom categories with your own emoji and color
- Navigate between months to look back at previous spending

All data stays in your browser (localStorage). Nothing is sent anywhere.

---

Tech

Plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no build step, no dependencies. The charts are hand-drawn SVG. The whole thing is one file.

---

Running it locally

Download `index.html` and open it in a browser. That's it.

(Optional) "Add to Home Screen" on mobile for an app-like experience.
